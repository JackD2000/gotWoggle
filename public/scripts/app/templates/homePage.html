Race Night Friday 31st May

We are having a race night on Friday 31st May and would love you, your friends & family to come along. The race night and disco will be from 7.30pm till Midnight. There will be a licensed bar, so it’s an over 18s event.

Tickets are £5.00 each and can be purchased via the leaders. If you are not able to come along on the night but would like to support us we are selling the jockeys and owners in the races, in advance of the evening, for £2.00 each.

We would also appreciate any donations for raffle prizes, these can be given to any of the leaders.

We really hope you can make it on the night and help support the Scout Group. If you need any more information please contact us.

What is Scouting?

The Scout Movement is a global co-educational youth organisation, the largest in the world, with over 28 million Members in 216 countries and territories.

The Scout Association offers everyday adventure and activity to almost 400,000 young people across the UK. Scouting is open to all young people aged 6 to 25 of every faith and background.
